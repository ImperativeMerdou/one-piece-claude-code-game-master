## Merdo's trajectory constraint

**To feel canon-accurate, Merdo's arc pacing must:**
1. **Stay under 100M bounty until AT LEAST 4-5 more arcs past Tessara.** Canon Luffy hits 100M after defeating Crocodile (effectively Warlord-tier). Merdo should NOT outpace Luffy in bounty — that's timeline-breaking.
2. **Arrive at Sabaody around 12 months from now.** Need at least 6-8 major island arcs between now and Sabaody. Tessara is #3. So: 5-6 more arcs.
3. **Not gain Conqueror's or Gear-5-equivalent breakthroughs before major arcs earn them.** The first Conqueror's burst should be a saga-defining moment, earned through compounding pressure.
4. **NOT encounter Yonko, Admirals, or Warlords at equal footing.** Brief chase scenes, glimpses, dispatch-cuts are fine. Direct combat = off-canon until much later.

## Canon events Merdo CANNOT prevent

Set this expectation clearly. The following WILL happen. Merdo can witness, react, be shaped, but he does not change these:

- Whitebeard War / Marineford (Ace dies)
- The Straw Hats' 2-year separation (Kuma scatters them)
- The Reverie (Warlord system abolished eventually)
- Punk Hazard, Dressrosa (canonical arcs happen regardless)
- Wano (Kaido will fall, but not to Merdo)
- Im's eventual reveal
- Laugh Tale exists

Merdo may witness these, be marked by them, even inherit things from them — but he does not replace canonical events. If the user wants the story to BEND canon, that's a conscious saga-level decision for discussion, not a DM-improvised change.
