**Reference file.** Read via the Read tool when the DM needs a named-attack name mid-fight and nothing player-supplied applies. Library is NOT loaded every session.

## MERDO — The Three Tiers

Merdo's personal fighting style is called **Danzai** (判罪, "judgment") — the battlefield as the one place where nothing can be faked. Three tiers, escalating by weight. Rule of one named attack per fight, max two per scene, cap tier by arc weight.

### TIER 1 — DANZAI EIGHT TRIGRAMS (八卦, *Hakke*) — MEDIUM STRIKES

Eight base patterns. Merdo's everyday committed techniques. Format: `**"DANZAI EIGHT TRIGRAMS — [NAME]!"**` or short `**"HAKKE — [NAME]!"**` when pace is tight. Shouted at commitment, not during measuring exchanges.

The eight (each a domain of battlefield force):

1. **VERDICT** — clean overhead with Judgment (kanabo); the judgment-stroke, line-of-execution.
2. **GUILTY-FOOT** — sweep takedown; foot-strike that decides a round.
3. **GALLOWS-WEIGHT** — pinning downward smash; opponent already falling meets the ground faster.
4. **CHAIN-MEASURE** — Gnadenkette flail horizontal arc; distance-control.
5. **WITNESS-GRIP** — gauntlet clinch into strike; close-work judgment.
6. **LAST-WORD** — committed straight punch, short travel, spiked gauntlet; ends conversations.
7. **BREAKING-POINT** — shoulder/body-check; sends the opponent through a wall or railing.
8. **IRON-GATE** — guard-and-answer; absorb a hit, return it at the same vector.

Deploy: routine committed strikes in a real fight. No Eight Trigrams name during sparring / light contact / measuring beats. Caps: max two Eight Trigrams names in a single fight scene unless the fight is extended multi-round.

### TIER 2 — CONQUEROR'S WILL — HEAVY NAMED ATTACKS (Point-of-No-Return)

Haki-coated, scale-declaring. Fired at ideology-fight commitment or against a serious opponent. Format: `**"CONQUEROR'S WILL — [NAME]!"**` or the bare shout `**"CONQUEROR'S WILL!!"**` when the name IS the technique.

- **CONQUEROR'S WILL — TOMB-MARKER** — the overhead with Conqueror's-coated Judgment; crater opens where the opponent's chest used to be. Merdo's most-seen heavy strike.
- **CONQUEROR'S WILL — NO PARDON** — Haki-coated bare-gauntlet finish. No weapon. For opponents he wants to beat personally.
- **CONQUEROR'S WILL — LAST RITES** — Gnadenkette flail full arc with Conqueror's coating; demolition-scale, structure-damage.
- **CONQUEROR'S WILL — RORORORO** — signature-laugh fused into a strike. Rare. Ideology-fight only.
- **CONQUEROR'S WILL!!** (bare shout) — the will BEFORE the technique; use when the player commits without a specific attack name, or when the moment is larger than any single move.

Cap: one CONQUEROR'S WILL per fight. Day 11 rookie tier: this is the current ceiling. Arc-fight finishers come from this tier.

### TIER 3 — DIVINE SUNDERER — ULTIMATE (Saga-Scale)

Reserved. Saga-late register. One fight per saga, maximum. Merdo has not yet named a DIVINE SUNDERER technique — it emerges when it fires. Do NOT pre-deploy in Tessara. Do NOT deploy in Day 11. This is the move the world has not yet seen. Its eventual deployment is the moment the bounty doubles and the epithet lands.

Thematic seed (build-only, do not narrate): DIVINE SUNDERER is the answer to Hadrian's Table. Whatever Merdo fires at the end of saga-peak, against a throne-holder, will wear that name for the first and sometimes only time.

### Activation register

Merdo is **SHOUTED** register (per fight.md §8). He yells his techniques at commit. No whispering, no silence-register. Eight Trigrams names can be snarled or shouted; CONQUEROR'S WILL is always shouted at full volume; DIVINE SUNDERER is a scream.

### Legacy mapping (for continuity only — do not reference in prose)

Old catalog strings that may appear in archived session logs or legacy files map cleanly to the new grammar:

- old "DANZAI: TOMB-MARKER" → **CONQUEROR'S WILL — TOMB-MARKER** (promoted to heavy tier)
- old "DANZAI: GUILTY-FOOT" → **DANZAI EIGHT TRIGRAMS — GUILTY-FOOT** (medium tier, retained)
- old "GNADENKETTE: LAST RITES" → **CONQUEROR'S WILL — LAST RITES**
- old "BARE-HANDED: NO PARDON" → **CONQUEROR'S WILL — NO PARDON**

## SUMI (Kami Kami no Mi — Paper Paper)

**Pattern:** Origami / scroll / paper craft vocabulary, often shouted with full kid-energy. CAPS frequent.

- **"KAMI KAMI: CRANE STRIKE!"** (turns to paper, refolds airborne, lands as a thousand cuts)
- **"KAMI KAMI: SCROLL DRAG!"** (wraps opponent in paper, weaponized origami)
- **"KAMI KAMI: BANNER TOSS!"** (sends crew above the crowd as a paper flag)
- **"PIRATE QUEEN'S MARK!"** (her own invention, mechanic varies, making it up)
- Weakness: rain. Names get sad and quiet in rain. *"Kami kami: ...soggy."*

## Foreign-language format reminder

When using a non-English style-name, format original first, translation in parens immediately:

> **"Higure: Yuukoku."** (Higure: Twilight Sorrow.)
> **"Kami Kami no… Senbazuru."** (Paper Paper… Thousand Cranes.)
> **"Rokushiki: Soru."** (Six Powers: Shave.)

Oda does it in the manga via furigana. Sparingly — not every name.
