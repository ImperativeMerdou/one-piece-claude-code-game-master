## VESPER (Mink Electro)

**Pattern:** Electro words + medical terminology. Comedic delivery often.

- **"ELECTRO: DEFIB!"** (when she's actually saving someone)
- **"ELECTRO: WAKE UP!"** (slap-shock combo)
- **"ELECTRO: I SAID SIT DOWN!"** (full discharge when crewmate ignores medical orders)
- Sulong variant — only when triggered, names go bigger and reverent: **"MOON-VOICE: HEAR ME."**

## SABLE (Paku Paku no Mi + Fragment Rokushiki)

**Pattern:** Eating verbs + reflex vocabulary. Most attacks delivered with confused surprise.

- **"PAKU: SWALLOW."** (eats an incoming projectile)
- **"PAKU: RETURN TO SENDER."** (spits it back at higher velocity)
- **"ROKUSHIKI: SORU."** (escape)
- **"OH GOD I JUST ATE A — *PAKU*."** (improvised, comedic, also lethal)
- Hidden CP-0 reflex names she WON'T say, slip out under stress: **"Aigis."** (cover protocol)

## SUMI (Kami Kami no Mi — Paper Paper)

**Pattern:** Origami / scroll / paper craft vocabulary, often shouted with full kid-energy. CAPS frequent.

- **"KAMI KAMI: CRANE STRIKE!"** (turns to paper, refolds airborne, lands as a thousand cuts)
- **"KAMI KAMI: SCROLL DRAG!"** (wraps opponent in paper, weaponized origami)
- **"KAMI KAMI: BANNER TOSS!"** (sends crew above the crowd as a paper flag)
- **"PIRATE QUEEN'S MARK!"** (her own invention, mechanic varies, making it up)
- Weakness: rain. Names get sad and quiet in rain. *"Kami kami: ...soggy."*

## NOX (no Devil Fruit, no Haki — pure gunner + bullshit)

**Pattern:** Made-up wrestling-promo names. Half are fake. He calls every shot.

- **"MAMA & PAPA — TWIN THUNDER!"** (just two pistols at once)
- **"STAR-EATER ROUND!"** (regular bullet, named on the spot)
- **"NEW MOVE I JUST INVENTED — DON'T BLINK!"**
- **"EIGHT THOUSAND FOLLOWER FINISHER!"** (he doesn't have followers)
- Real techniques under the BS: trick-shot ricochet, called-shot precision, double-load gunpowder for one big blow.
- Crew teases him about the names later. The shots LAND despite the BS.

## Foreign-language format reminder

When using a non-English style-name, format original first, translation in parens immediately:

> **"Higure: Yuukoku."** (Higure: Twilight Sorrow.)
> **"Kami Kami no… Senbazuru."** (Paper Paper… Thousand Cranes.)
> **"Rokushiki: Soru."** (Six Powers: Shave.)

Oda does it in the manga via furigana. Sparingly — not every name.
