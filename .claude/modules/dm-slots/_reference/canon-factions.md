## Canon Factions — Who's Active, Where, Doing What

The political/military landscape of the One Piece world at Merdo's current position (Day 11, post-Alabasta-news). Use this when writing meanwhile-cuts, News Coo entries, character intel.

---

## The World Government

**Structure:** Five Elders (Gorosei) at the top, unnamed publicly. Celestial Dragons (Tenryubito) above all law. Marines enforce. Cipher Pol investigate + assassinate. Science Division (Vegapunk) experiment.

**Current plot beats:**
- Sengoku (Fleet Admiral) managing post-Alabasta fallout. Reports on Crocodile defeat flooding his desk.
- The Warlord seat Crocodile held is vacant. Recruiting for replacement (will eventually be Blackbeard after Marineford; pre-then: nobody).
- Garp (Vice Admiral) rotating between HQ and tracking his grandson Luffy informally.
- Admirals active: **Akainu** (Sakazuki — "Red Dog") hardline Absolute Justice. **Aokiji** (Kuzan — "Blue Pheasant") lazy moral. **Kizaru** (Borsalino — "Yellow Monkey") casual cruel. One of these will eventually appear in a Merdo arc.

**Merdo's crew visibility to WG:** Currently low (rookie). Ashren's Lunarian reveal = escalation trigger (see below).

## Cipher Pol

**CP-0 (Aigis 0) — Mary Geoise, directly under Celestial Dragons.** Top-tier assassin-agents. Masks. Rumored hooded figures. Not yet active against Merdo.

**CP-9 — Water 7 / Enies Lobby cover identity.** Rob Lucci, Kaku, Jabra, Blueno, Kalifa, Fukurou, Kumadori, Spandam. Will surface in ~6 months. Sable's history was CP-0, not CP-9, but the organizations know each other.

**CP-6, CP-7, CP-8 etc.** — lesser intel cells. Regional.

**Rook's sealed file is held by:** Marine Intelligence Division (which feeds both Marine HQ and CPs as needed).

**Sable's CP-0 file:** Rob Lucci filed her as "compromised asset." Saved by the bureaucracy — technically she's on standby, not marked for elimination. If she surfaces with Merdo publicly, this changes.

## The Revolutionary Army

**Monkey D. Dragon — HQ at Baltigo.** Father of Luffy (not yet public knowledge — Dragon revealed chapter 432).

**Senior officers:** Emporio Ivankov, Inazuma, Dragon's inner circle. Kuma (secretly).

**Current activity:** Expanding, toppling small WG-aligned regimes. Moves slow. Not currently targeting Merdo but if Merdo publicly attacks WG/Marine infrastructure, Revolutionary Army MAY make contact.

**Potential ally for Merdo's ideology (drag every throne into the dirt) — but ethical overlap ≠ operational overlap.** The Revolutionaries have their own agenda.

Don't pre-activate the Void-Century meaning; let it surface through earned play.

## Canon-relevant civilian factions

**The Kuja (Amazon Lily) — female-only warrior pirate society.** Hancock's crew. Not encountered yet.

**The Minks (Zou) — animal-hybrid tribe.** Vesper's people-at-large. Sulong under the full moon. Currently under pressure (Wano-adjacent politics, not yet Jack's attack).

**The Fish-Men / Merfolk (Fish-Man Island) — beneath the Red Line.** Volo's culture. Jinbei's home. Arlong's origin. Currently in political tension pre-New Fish-Man Pirates arc.

**The Giants (Elbaf) — North Blue.** Dorry, Brogy. Giants respect combat and oaths. Merdo's great-grandfather stories would ORIGINATE here.

**The Lunarians — extinct-or-hunted.** King is canonically the only known survivor in the New World. Ashren on Merdo's crew is a SECOND known survivor — canon-expanding but WG-alarming.
