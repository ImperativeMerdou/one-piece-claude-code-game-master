# Haki Tiers — Operational Ladder

Companion to `haki-training-arc.md` (which covers the dramatic shape of LEARNING Haki). This module covers the LADDER — when a fighter qualifies for which tier, what each looks like in prose, and what counters each.

When the DM needs to call a Haki beat in scene, pull from here. No improvising tiers mid-fight.

---

## The three colors

One Piece Haki splits into three named colors. Most fighters who train have one or two; only a tiny minority manifest all three. Each color has BASIC and ADVANCED forms. A few have a recognized THIRD stage.

| Color | Basic | Advanced | Top |
|---|---|---|---|
| Observation (Kenbunshoku) | Sense intent | Future Sight | — |
| Armament (Busoshoku) | Coating | Internal Destruction (Ryuou) | Color-bearing (Black) |
| Conqueror's (Haoshoku) | Knock out weak-willed | Selective sparing | Coating (with black lightning) |

The colors do NOT power-up uniformly. A fighter can be advanced-Observation but basic-Armament and have no Conqueror's at all. Treat each color as its own track.

---

## Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku) — sensing

### Basic
- Feel a person's emotional intent before they speak.
- Sense presence in the next room, around a corner, behind a door.
- Count enemies in a building from outside.
- Sense killing intent — identify the threat in a crowd.

**Who**: most committed fighters with training. Rookie Sanji, post-skip Nami in calm conditions.
**Counter**: a Marine without intent (drugged, hypnotized, sleepwalking) will not register. A trap (mechanical, no mind) will not register.

### Advanced (Future Sight / Mantra in Skypiea)
- Glimpse opponent's next move as a flicker an instant before it happens.
- Dodge attacks without seeing them.
- Read a fight by anticipation, not reaction.

**Who**: Katakuri, post-skip Luffy (mid-Whole-Cake onward), Skypiea Enel ("Mantra"), Rayleigh, Shanks. **Rare** — top-tier or top-rookie only.
**Counter**: emotional destabilization. Katakuri loses Future Sight when his composure breaks. A user who is enraged, grieving, or shocked drops to basic.
**Display tells**: brief unfocused stare, lips moving silently, a half-step taken before the strike arrives.

### Beyond
Some users develop **range and density** — Conqueror's-class generals can sense the entire battlefield. Treat as Advanced + experience, not a separate tier.

## Armament Haki (Busoshoku) — armor and offense

### Basic (Coating)
- Invisible "iron skin" over a body part.
- Lets you TOUCH and damage Logia users (the canonical Logia counter).
- Can be coated onto weapons (sword, jitte, fist).
- Can block named attacks at parity tier.

**Who**: every serious New World fighter. By the time crews enter the Grand Line second half, basic Armament is table-stakes for officer-tier.
**Cost**: stamina. Cannot stay coated indefinitely.
**Display tells**: in story: occasional black sheen on the strike. In prose: "his fist connected with Crocodile's chest and Crocodile flinched — there had been no sand to scatter."

### Advanced (Internal Destruction / Ryuou — "Flowing Form")
- Bypass armor (organic OR worn) and deliver shock to internal organs.
- Touch a fish-man's outer arm and break their inner ribs.
- Damage a Devil-Fruit user's body without affecting the protective elemental form.
- Used by Rayleigh on Roswald's slave (knocks him down without touching him visibly).

**Who**: Vivi's father Cobra (yes — internal destruction lineage), Rayleigh, post-skip Luffy, Hyogoro of the Wano. Mastered version of basic Armament.
**Display tells**: the strike doesn't visibly land hard, but the target collapses — internal bleeding, broken ribs from a tap. **In prose: the silence after the touch is the tell.**

### Color-bearing (Black Armament / "Hardening")
- Visible BLACK coating — the limb turns metallic-black.
- Required to scratch a Yonko-tier opponent.
- Highest defensive form; can clash with Conqueror's-coated strikes without disintegrating the user.
- Often combined with Internal Destruction.

**Who**: top-tier only. Yonko commanders, Admirals when they bother, post-Wano top rookies.
**Display tells**: the limb is BLACK. Other characters notice and react. The narrator does NOT explain — let the visual do it.

## Conqueror's Haki (Haoshoku) — domination

The rarest. Roughly 1 in millions has it. CANNOT be trained from nothing — you have or you don't. The TRAINING is in CONTROL, not in acquisition.

### Basic (Crowd-Knockout)
- Wills the weak-willed to lose consciousness.
- Affects everyone in line of sight whose will is below the user's gap-threshold.
- A fighter with stronger will than the Conqueror's user is unaffected.
- Users without control hit allies and bystanders too.

**Who**: Luffy (East Blue, untrained — accidentally surfaces during Duval encounter), Shanks (controls his at command), Whitebeard, Big Mom, Kaido, Roger, Rayleigh, Doflamingo, Ace, Sanji-no-but-his-mother-yes (Reiju mention), Boa Hancock, Chinjao, Don Chinjao, Katakuri, Yamato, Zoro (post-Wano confirmed).
**Cost**: dramatic stamina drain when used widely. Concentration to spare specific targets.
**Display tells**: characters around the user collapse. The user's expression turns iron. Sometimes a literal aura — heat-distortion in the air, ground cracks. **In prose: name 2-3 specific NPCs who go down, name the one who stays standing because their will matched the user's.**

### Intermediate (Selective Sparing)
- Can choose WHO drops and who stays awake.
- Crucial for crowd-scene combat where the user has allies present.
- Distinguishes a user who has TRAINED Conqueror's from one who only HAS it.

**Who**: Big Mom (almost always selective at her tier), Kaido, Shanks, Roger, post-skip Luffy.

### Advanced (Conqueror's Coating)
- The user coats a strike — fist, sword, weapon — in **visible black lightning**.
- Damage WITHOUT TOUCHING. The strike connects through air, through walls, through distance.
- Reserved for the very top: Yonko, Yonko-rivals, post-Wano Luffy, post-Wano Zoro, Roger and Whitebeard in flashback.
- Required to wound a Yonko-tier opponent meaningfully.

**Who**: Roger, Whitebeard, Shanks, Kaido, Big Mom, Rayleigh, post-Wano Luffy, post-Wano Zoro, Mihawk (implied), Garp (implied).
**Display tells**: BLACK LIGHTNING visible on the strike. **In prose: name the lightning. Let it crack against the air. The target's coating breaks AROUND the strike before the strike lands.**

### Conqueror's Clash
When two Conqueror's-coated strikes collide, the **sky splits**. Visible cloud-rupture and shockwave for kilometers. Witnesses on other islands feel it. Used as the visual signature of WORLD-CLASS combat.

Examples in canon: Roger vs Whitebeard (Edd War), Luffy vs Kaido (rooftop). Each only happens ONCE per saga — overuse drains the meaning.

## When Haki cannot be used

- **Sea Prism Stone contact**: all Haki suppressed alongside DF. The user is reduced to trained body.
- **Heavy emotional destabilization**: Observation drops to basic; Armament can still flow but with less control; Conqueror's can SURGE uncontrolled (worst-case).
- **Severe injury / concussion**: Future Sight cuts out. The fighter is in raw reaction mode.
- **Sleep / unconsciousness / drug**: all Haki off.
- **Fatigue stack**: each color drains its own pool. A long fight can leave Armament intact but Observation dead.

## Calling Haki in narration

**Don't say "he used Armament Haki"** — that's stat speech. Say:

- "His fist hit the sand-skin. The sand resisted. His fist did not stop."
- "Hudore's eyes unfocused for a second. Merdo's right cross was already moving — and Hudore had stepped left before it left the shoulder."
- "The rival captain's voice broke the air. Three of the Marines went down where they stood. Two stayed up. He looked at those two." (Conqueror's Haki demonstrated by whoever earns the scene.)
- "The strike never reached. It cracked black across the gap, and Yann's sleeve burst at the wrist."

The COLOR is in WHAT THE READER SEES, not in a label.
