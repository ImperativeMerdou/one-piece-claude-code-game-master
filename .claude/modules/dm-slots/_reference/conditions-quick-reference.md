## Conditions Quick Reference — One Piece / Shonen Version

**Rewritten for the Merdo Pirates campaign.** D&D's core conditions mostly apply in a shonen register; a few One Piece-specific ones are added. Conditions are DM-internal mechanics; narrate them through the BODY and the WORLD, not the condition name.

---

## Universal conditions (still apply)

| Condition | Effect (DM-internal) | Narrate as (player-facing) |
|---|---|---|
| **Blinded** | Can't see, auto-fail sight checks, disadvantage on attacks | "He can't see through the dust / smoke / flash — his strikes go wide." |
| **Deafened** | Can't hear, auto-fail hearing checks | "The explosion left a ringing — he's reading lips." |
| **Frightened** | Disadvantage while source in sight, can't move closer | Show the BODY — the flinch that doesn't happen, the step that goes the wrong way. Never just "he's scared." |
| **Grappled** | Speed 0 in the grip | "The Oni's hand is around his arm. He's not walking out of this until he breaks it." |
| **Incapacitated** | Can't take actions or reactions | Show WHY — knocked breath out of him, took a shot to the temple, paralyzed, rag-dolled. |
| **Paralyzed** | Incapacitated + can't move/speak, auto-fail STR/DEX saves | "His legs won't answer. His jaw won't answer. He's aware. That's the problem." |
| **Poisoned** | Disadvantage on attacks and ability checks | SPECIFIC poison (Melaina's Medusa venom vs. Alabasta scorpion vs. Tessara pit-fighter blade). Each is its own scene. |
| **Prone** | Disadvantage on attacks, melee attacks have advantage vs. him | "He's down. The floor is in his mouth." |
| **Restrained** | Speed 0, disadvantage on attacks and DEX saves | Show the BINDING (chain, Sumi's paper wrap, Seastone cuff, Kairoseki-lined net). |
| **Stunned** | Incapacitated + can't move, can barely speak | "He's standing. He's not there. Next beat is the DM's or an ally's." |
| **Unconscious** | Incapacitated + can't move/speak, unaware, attacks auto-crit | The scene's anchor shifts to whoever's awake. |

## One Piece-specific conditions

| Condition | Effect | Common source | Narrate as |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Seastone-weakened** | Devil Fruit user loses all fruit powers + half speed + disadvantage on everything physical | Seastone cuffs (Marine kit), Seastone weapon contact, Seastone floor (specific prisons), Impel Down materials | Show the WEIGHT. "The fruit is gone. He's just a man, and the weight is unbearable." |
| **Water-exposed** (partial) | Devil Fruit user at half strength/speed while wet | Rain, seawater spray, forced submersion | Fruit user IS weaker. Body shows it — labored breath, dragging feet. |
| **Water-submerged** (full) | Devil Fruit user CANNOT ACT — can't move limbs, can't use fruit | Falling in the sea, Fish-Man Karate pull, being held under | This is a KILL state. The crew acts to save, or the DF user drowns. |
| **Haki-struck (Armament)** | Intangible fruit users can be HIT; Logia can be damaged; applies to the strike only | Armament Haki user lands a blow | Show the COLOR shift — black-coated strike, the flinch of the fruit user realizing they CAN be hurt. |
| **Conqueror's-stunned** | Weaker-willed combatants knocked out or frozen | Conqueror's release | Show the ROOM before the target — the civilians drop, the weaker fighters faint, only the worthy stay standing. |
| **Petrified (Medusa-style)** | Partial or full stone — speed 0, eventually dies if not reversed | Melaina's full Medusa form (she has only fired this once, badly), Boa Hancock at canon tier | Show the STONE spreading — fingertip, forearm, shoulder. A clock. |
| **Paper-soaked** | Kami Kami fruit (Sumi) cannot activate — paper body is ruined by water | Rain, any liquid contact | "The paper is SOAKED. She's back to just a kid with two swords." (Hard weakness, must be tracked during rain arcs.) |
| **Flame-burned** (Lunarian) | Burn damage + visible scarring + lingering heat in tissue | Ashren's back-flame at full deploy, canon Lunarian encounters | Lunarian flame burns DIFFERENT — silver-white or gold-tinted, hotter than natural fire. Damage carries into later scenes. |
| **Sulong** (Mink-boosted) | Mink with full-moon access transforms: 3x speed, 2x strength, temporary but catastrophic | Full moon visible to a Mink, Vesper saga-late | Vesper has NEVER triggered this. When it happens it will be saga-weight. Narrate as OVERWHELMING. |
| **Electro-shocked** (Mink) | Stunned for 1 round per hit + minor damage | Mink Electro strike (Vesper) | Show the AMBER arc — Vesper's Electro is amber-tinted, not standard blue-white. |
| **Gear-exhausted** (brawler) | Character who pushed past baseline recovery tier — massive debuff until proper rest | Post-heart-peak override per `emotion-will-heart.md` | Show the COLLAPSE. Luffy post-Bajrang-Gun. Saga-late for Merdo. |
| **Awakening-cost** (advanced fruit user) | Awakened fruit user pays body or lifespan or sanity cost | Doflamingo string-awakening, Zoan awakening absorb risk | Saga-late. Not applicable for current crew. |

## Charmed (replaced in this campaign)

D&D's **Charmed** condition doesn't cleanly map to One Piece — there's no basic "charm person" spell. Related One Piece equivalents:

- **Captivated by beauty** (Boa Hancock's Mero Mero no Mi — canon) — target petrified by attraction, ONLY if they ARE attracted (asexual or pure-hearted targets immune). This is saga-late for the crew.
- **Possessed / controlled** (Doflamingo strings on bodies) — target's body moves against their will. Saga-late.
- **Compelled by Conqueror's influence** — a selective Conqueror's user can KNOCK OUT targets but not truly control them. Not a charm effect.

For general social "charmed" fiction: use `villain-kit.md` MANIPULATION section (false intimacy, co-option, weaponized vulnerability). That's the shonen substitute.

## Exhaustion — merged into `emotion-will-heart.md` POST-COMBAT STATE

D&D's 6-level exhaustion ladder is replaced by the 5-tier post-combat state system in `emotion-will-heart.md`:

| Old D&D exhaustion | New shonen tier |
|---|---|
| Level 1-2 (disadvantage, half speed) | **Winded** |
| Level 3 (disadvantage on attacks/saves) | **Tired** |
| Level 4 (HP max halved) | **Spent** |
| Level 5 (speed 0) | **Broken** |
| Level 6 (death) | **Catastrophic** (saga-weight) |

Use the tiers from `emotion-will-heart.md`. Don't track both systems.

## What NOT to do

- DON'T narrate condition names to the player. "You are Blinded" is wrong. "The dust blew into his eyes and he couldn't see the blade coming" is right.
- DON'T apply multiple conditions at once mechanically in a way that stacks into unplayable combos. Pick the dominant condition for this beat and narrate IT.
- DON'T forget Seastone. If a Devil Fruit user enters a Marine cell, Impel Down, or an enemy ship with Seastone floors, track it. The fruit is GONE.
- DON'T skip water effects for Devil Fruit users in fights on decks during rain, at harbors, in bathhouses. Sable, Sumi, Melaina, Kara all have water vulnerability to varying degrees.
- DON'T over-deploy One Piece-specific conditions at rookie-tier. Petrified (Medusa) has fired ONCE badly. Conqueror's-stunned is saga-late. Sulong is saga-late. Keep the scale honest.
