# Reward Calibration — Small Wins, Often

TTRPG players need MORE FREQUENT rewards than anime watchers. Anime audience waits 50 episodes because they watch weekly with other things to do. A TTRPG player invested an hour — he needs to feel something won, gained, or progressed BEFORE the session ends.

The saga has late goodies (D. clan, Conqueror's, Dragon Fruit, Shanks-Melaina, Joy Boy) — but those are far. The player needs midstream wins.

Arc-specific planted rewards live in `ACTIVE_PLANTS.md` (hot) and `chekhov-rack.json` (full). Don't duplicate those lists here.

---

## REWARD TIERS

### TIER 1 — micro-rewards (2–4 per session)

Tiny "I gained something" moments. Cost the DM nothing; make the player feel SEEN.

- **Crew bond moment** — Vesper passes him a bandage he didn't ask for; Sable's shoulder brushes his at the railing; Sumi shows him a drawing
- **NPC recognition** — a one-shot character remembers Merdo's name, defers, asks for advice
- **Reputation flicker** — overheard: "Hear about that horned one?"; stallholder gives a deal
- **Skill recognition** — "you've fought before"; Mako mimics a technique
- **Discovery** — Merdo notices what the crew didn't (pit instincts)
- **Comedy win** — running gag lands (Mango Conspiracy callback)
- **World-acknowledges-you** — passing sailor tips hat; Marine looks twice; child points

### TIER 2 — small wins (1–2 per session)

Concrete in-game gain the player notices.

- **Item gained** — gear, a coin, a folded note
- **Information gained** — intel, a name overheard, faction rumor confirmed
- **Crew development** — small breakthrough (Sumi names a sword; Nox admits a real shot count; Vesper goes 24h without biting)
- **Skill micro-progression** — Armament flickered slightly easier; Bjorn Stance held three seconds longer
- **Public moment** — bookmaker chalked Merdo's name with new respect; crowd parted
- **Reputation tick** — News Coo carried a half-line; a port whispered his name

### TIER 3 — arc rewards (1–3 per arc)

Medium-payoff. Visible, named, tracked.

- Bounty issued / raised — first bounty, first jump, first epithet
- Named-attack debuted publicly
- New crew member confirmed
- Major NPC defeated / spared
- Faction relationship shift — Donquixote files a real report; Adaam Goroa sends a letter
- Crew bond major moment — crewmate declares something to/about Merdo
- Plant fires — an `ACTIVE_PLANTS.md` / rack item lands

### TIER 4 — saga rewards (rare, earned)

Conqueror's first burst · The D. revealed · Dragon Fruit found/refused/lost · Yonko encounter survived · major canon-character meeting.

---

## DEPLOYMENT RHYTHM

Per session target:
- **2–4 Tier 1**
- **1–2 Tier 2**
- **0–1 Tier 3**
- **0 Tier 4** unless the moment GENUINELY arrives

Zero rewards in a session = problem. All Tier 1 = nothing moving; slip in a Tier 2.

---

## FRAMING — make it LAND

When a reward fires, FRAME IT so the player notices. Don't bury it in a paragraph. Give it its own beat.

**Wrong (buried):**
> Merdo crossed the dock and overheard Partho the bookmaker mention his name to a client, then walked past a fisherman who tipped his hat, then continued to the colosseum gate.

**Right (surfaced):**
> Partho's voice carried across the floor: "Yeah — the horned one. Two-to-one against. People are betting against him."
>
> Behind him, a fisherman tipped his hat once as Merdo passed. Didn't say anything. Just the tip.
