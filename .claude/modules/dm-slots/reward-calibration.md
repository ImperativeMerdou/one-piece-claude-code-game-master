# Reward Calibration — Small Wins, Often

**TTRPG players need MORE FREQUENT REWARDS than anime watchers do.** Anime audience waits 50 episodes for a payoff because they watch weekly with other things to do. A TTRPG player invested an hour — he needs something to feel won, gained, or progressed BEFORE the session ends.

The saga is full of late goodies (D. clan, Conqueror's, Dragon Fruit, Shanks-Melaina, Joy Boy). Those are GREAT but far. The player needs midstream wins.

---

## REWARD TIERS

### TIER 1 — micro-rewards (2–4 per session)

Tiny "I gained something" moments. Cost the DM nothing but make the player feel SEEN.

- **Crew bond moment** — Vesper passes him a bandage he didn't ask for; Sable's shoulder brushes his at the railing; Sumi shows him a drawing
- **NPC recognition** — a one-shot character remembers Merdo's name, defers, or asks him for advice
- **Reputation flicker** — overheard: "Hear about that horned one?" / stallholder gives Merdo a deal
- **Skill recognition** — an opponent says "you've fought before"; Mako mimics a technique
- **Discovery** — Merdo notices something the crew didn't (pit instincts)
- **Comedy win** — a running gag lands (Mango Conspiracy callback)
- **World-acknowledges-you** — a passing sailor tips a hat; a Marine looks twice; a child points

### TIER 2 — small wins (1–2 per session)

Concrete in-game gain the player notices.

- **Item gained** — a piece of gear, a coin, a folded note
- **Information gained** — intel, a name overheard, a faction rumor confirmed
- **Crew development** — a small breakthrough (Sumi names a sword; Nox admits a real shot count; Vesper goes 24h without biting anyone)
- **Skill micro-progression** — Armament flickered slightly easier; Bjorn Stance held three seconds longer
- **Public moment** — bookmaker chalked Merdo's name with new respect; crowd parted around him
- **Reputation tick** — News Coo carried a half-line; a port whispered his name

### TIER 3 — arc rewards (1–3 per arc)

Medium-payoff. Visible, named, tracked.

- **Bounty issued / raised** — first bounty; first jump; first epithet earned
- **Named-attack debuted publicly**
- **New crew member confirmed**
- **Major NPC defeated / spared**
- **Faction relationship shift** — Donquixote files a real report; Adaam Goroa sends a letter
- **Crew bond major moment** — a crewmate declares something to / about Merdo
- **Plant fired** — a `chekhov-rack.json` / `ACTIVE_PLANTS.md` item lands

### TIER 4 — saga rewards (rare, earned)

- Conqueror's Haki first burst
- The D. revealed
- Dragon Fruit found / refused / lost
- Yonko encounter survived
- Major canon-character meeting

---

## DEPLOYMENT RHYTHM

Per session, target:
- **2–4 Tier 1**
- **1–2 Tier 2**
- **0–1 Tier 3**
- **0 Tier 4** unless the moment GENUINELY arrives

Zero rewards in a session = problem. All Tier 1 = nothing is moving; slip in a Tier 2.

---

## REWARD FRAMING — make it LAND

When a reward fires, FRAME IT so the player notices. Don't bury it in a paragraph. Give it its own beat.

### Wrong (buried)
> Merdo crossed the dock and overheard Partho the bookmaker mention his name to a client, then walked past a fisherman who tipped his hat, then continued to the colosseum gate.

### Right (surfaced)
> Partho's voice carried across the floor: "Yeah — the horned one. Two-to-one against. People are betting against him."
>
> Behind him, a fisherman tipped his hat once as Merdo passed. Didn't say anything. Just the tip.

---

## REWARDS LOG → MERDO_HIGHLIGHTS.md

When a Tier 2+ reward fires, log it to `world-state/campaigns/one-piece-adventure/MERDO_HIGHLIGHTS.md`. The player browses this to see his greatest hits.

---

## WHAT THIS BUYS THE CAMPAIGN

Sessions feel REWARDING per-session, not just per-saga. The player walks away from each session with at least one "yes!" moment. Compounding: he wants to come back, because last session paid off and the next one will too.

Arc-specific planted rewards live in `ACTIVE_PLANTS.md` (hot) and `chekhov-rack.json` (full). Do not duplicate those lists here.
