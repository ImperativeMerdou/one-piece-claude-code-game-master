## Canon Geography — Paradise Map + Merdo's Position

Where is Merdo? Relative to what canonical places? This module anchors the crew in real map-geography.

## The Grand Line architecture (canon)

- **Reverse Mountain** at the Red Line, where all four Blues converge and pour down into Paradise.
- **Paradise:** the first half of the Grand Line. ~26 islands along 7 parallel routes from Reverse Mountain to Sabaody Archipelago.
- **Red Line** bisects the world vertically. Mary Geoise on top.
- **Fish-Man Island** beneath the Red Line at the halfway point.
- **New World:** the second half of the Grand Line. Yonko territory.
- **Laugh Tale** at the end of the New World. Endpoint.

The **Calm Belt** flanks the Grand Line on both sides, full of Sea Kings. Impassable without seastone-coated ships.

The **Seven routes through Paradise** each pass different islands before converging at Sabaody. Canon references the routes but doesn't rank them. The islands vary wildly — some magnetic anomalies, some climate extremes, some political anomalies, some Sea-King-rich.

## Merdo's route — NOT "the Worst Route"

**Correction from earlier framing:** drop the "Worst Route" branding. It's fanfic overclaim.

Reframe: Merdo's crew drew **one of the seven starting routes from Reverse Mountain**. This particular route has been harder than standard — magnetic anomalies bringing harder islands in closer sequence, fewer safe ports, Sea-King-dense waters. The crew speaks of "our route" or "the rough route" or "our stretch" — NOT "the Worst Route."

This is a CRAFT correction. Fanfic branding ("worst") implies narrative destiny. Canon framing ("rough stretch of one of seven routes") stays in-world.

## Using this file

When a meanwhile-cut fires, the DM should know WHERE it's cutting to. Use this file.

When the player asks "where are we?" — give them real geography, not vague "the Grand Line."

When inventing a new island arc — place it plausibly within Merdo's projected route. NOT in the middle of canon territory (don't put Merdo at Dressrosa; Doflamingo is there).
