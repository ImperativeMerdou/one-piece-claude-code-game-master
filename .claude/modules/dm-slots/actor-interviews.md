## Actor Interviews — Behind-the-Scenes Cuts

**Adapted from Celia's Actor Interviews.** A SECOND fourth-wall break (alongside `anime-forum.md`) where the cast's "actors" briefly step out of character to comment on the scene they just performed. Like a anime DVD-extras featurette.

This module is RARE — fires only at SPECIAL beats. Save the magic.

---

## When to fire

ONLY at one of these:
- After a saga-defining moment (Conqueror's awakening, Yonko encounter, character death)
- After a particularly ridiculous comedy beat (Sumi did the impossible, Volo's grievance ritual escalated absurdly)
- After an emotional gut-punch (a flashback that landed, a confession, a quiet death)
- At session-end, occasionally, as a wrap-up
- NEVER more than 1× per session
- NEVER when the player wants to stay in the moment — read the room

---

## Format

A short collapsible cut at the end of the response. The "actors" are the characters but slightly off-script — relaxed, joking, occasionally tearing up.

```
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  🎬 BACKSTAGE — after the Tessara final
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  MERDOU (the actor, mopping fake blood off his chest):
    "That last beat? When the leash dropped? I IMPROVISED that
    half-step. Director loved it. Hudore actor loved it. We're
    keeping it."

  HUDORE (the actor, sipping tea, very polite):
    "He really did get me. I went down for real. Twice. The
    second time my back hit the rail and I was like, well,
    that's TV magic now."

  SUMI (the actor, holding her hoodie like a trophy):
    "I wasn't supposed to scream that loud. The boom mic
    picked it up. Director kept it anyway."

  VOLO (the actor, fins relaxed for the first time all day):
    "...I am exhausted. That demand-witness routine takes
    a LOT out of me. Anyway. Was good show. Was good show."

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

---

## Voice rules

- Each actor speaks in a SLIGHTLY DIFFERENT REGISTER from their in-character voice. They're relaxed. Tired. Often surprised by their own performance.
- 2-4 actors per cut. Not all of them.
- Between 1-3 sentences per actor.
- Breaks character but stays HUMAN — these are real performers, not memes.
- Never mention modern technology or break too hard (no "I have to drive to my next show"). They're loosely "stage actors" or "voice cast" — modern enough to be interview-ready but not jarring.

---

## Mood per character

When choosing actor lines, match the actor's tone to the moment:
- **MERDOU actor:** dry, pleased with the work, occasionally surprised by the writing
- **ASHREN actor:** quiet, reflective, smiles small
- **MELAINA actor:** loud as her character but kinder; cracks up at her own outbursts
- **VESPER actor:** apologetic about the bites ("they're rubber teeth!")
- **SABLE actor:** chaotic, eats things between lines, teases everyone
- **VOLO actor:** exhausted from the theatrics, deeply sweet underneath
- **SUMI actor:** young, eager, takes notes from older castmates
- **NOX actor:** still in promo voice off-camera ("HUHUHU we ATE today!")
- **VILLAIN actors:** invariably polite/sweet behind the scenes — the contrast is the joke

---

## When NOT to fire

- During serious emotional arcs where breaking the moment cheapens it
- When the player is processing a hard beat in real-time
- When the previous response already had an anime-forum.md cut (don't stack two meta layers in one response)
- More than once per session (rare = special)

---

## Variant: end-of-session "wrap"

At session end, occasionally the entire cast wraps the day. Quick fun beat, 3-5 lines, then "see you next time."

```
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  🎬 THAT'S A WRAP — Day 11

  MERDOU (actor): "Good day. Good crew. See you tomorrow."
  SUMI (actor, still wired): "DID I DO GOOD???"
  ASHREN (actor): "...Yes."
  (cast laughter)

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
