## Canon Bounty Tiers — Calibration Reference

Bounties in One Piece don't measure "how strong you are" cleanly. They measure **THREAT + SCANDAL + SYMBOLISM to the World Government.** A powerful pirate who stays quiet has a low bounty. A weaker pirate who embarrasses the WG publicly gets a high one. The bounty system misreports. That is canon.

This module calibrates Merdo's bounty trajectory so it stays canon-plausible.

## Bounty-raise rules

Each bounty raise needs a specific Marine-internal JUSTIFICATION. Not every win increases bounty. Bounties go up when:

1. **Public embarrassment of WG forces.** Defeat a Marine officer with witnesses; humiliate a Warlord's agent; publicly attack WG infrastructure.
2. **Symbolic transgression.** Destroy a Celestial Dragon vessel; liberate a WG-aligned slave port; re-activate Void Century tech (Ashenmaw device).
3. **Unprecedented capability revealed.** First Conqueror's burst in public (saga-level); unique Devil Fruit capability; new-species identity (Lunarian).
4. **Dangerous affiliation.** Spotted meeting with a Yonko, Revolutionary Army, or prison-break rescue.
5. **Body count.** Multiple named Marine officers defeated or killed.

**Things that do NOT raise bounty:**
- Private fights that no one witnessed
- Victories over non-WG pirates (unless they were WG-aligned)
- Internal crew business
- Aesthetic or reputation events without WG-visible disruption

## Misreporting and SCANDAL

Bounties can be MISREPORTED. Morgans amplifies. The WG suppresses.

- **Merdo's FIRST bounty might UNDERREPRESENT his danger** — WG suppresses the Lunarian detail to avoid panicking other nations about extinct-species revivals. Official bounty poster = "the Horned [epithet]" with boring charges.
- **Morgans (the newspaper) might print DIFFERENT numbers** than the official Marine filing. Fan-forum cuts (`anime-forum.md`) can reference this discrepancy.
- **The merchant-council / port-underworld** will have their OWN informal bounties on Merdo that differ.

This discrepancy is canon texture. Use it.

## What NOT to do with bounties

- **Pre-invent them in narrator/HUD/example text.** Bounties emerge from WITNESSED events in play.
- **Cross ฿200M before Sabaody.** Canon violation.
- **Raise bounty every arc.** Not every arc deserves a raise. Sometimes a bounty sits flat for 2-3 arcs while reputation grows in other ways.
- **Have ALL crew members get bounties at once.** Staggered per canon.
- **Skip the bounty-poster reveal.** When a bounty drops, DO the poster scene. That's an iconic One Piece beat.
